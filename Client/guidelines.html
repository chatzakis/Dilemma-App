Community Guidelines

We encourage a respectful, inclusive, and thoughtful community where everyone can participate. Please follow these guidelines when submitting questions:

  • 1. Respectful Language

    Ensure your questions are respectful and free of offensive or derogatory language. Avoid using harmful terms related to race, gender, religion, nationality, or personal identity.

  • 2. Clarity and Relevance

    Submit clear, well-structured questions. Make sure the question offers two distinct, meaningful answers for users to choose from, and avoid vague or ambiguous questions.

  • 3. Avoid Sensitive or Inflammatory Topics

    Refrain from submitting questions about divisive topics such as politics or religion. Avoid content that may provoke conflict or encourage hate speech, harassment, or discrimination.

  • 4. No Explicit or Inappropriate Content

    Keep the platform safe for all users by avoiding adult, explicit, or violent content. Questions should adhere to community decency standards.

  • 5. Originality

    Submit original questions that foster interesting discussions. Avoid plagiarizing questions from others or external sources.

  • 6. Avoid Personal Attacks

    Do not submit questions that single out individuals or groups for criticism. Respect personal privacy and avoid sharing private or identifying information.

  • 7. No Advertising or Promotions

    Avoid using questions to promote products, services, or external websites. Do not submit spammy or promotional content.

  • 8. Be Inclusive

    Submit questions that can be enjoyed by a broad and diverse audience, and promote inclusive participation.

  • 9. Stay Positive and Engaging

    Create fun, interesting, and thought-provoking dilemmas that engage the community in positive ways. Encourage curiosity and meaningful interaction.

  • 10. Follow the Platform’s Terms of Service

    Adhere to the platform’s terms of service. Non-compliance may result in question removal or suspension.

By following these guidelines, we can create a safe, respectful, and fun environment for everyone!
